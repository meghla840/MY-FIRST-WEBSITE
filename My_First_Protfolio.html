Here are two projects that hold a special place in my journey:

📚 BookNest – A Simple eBook Web App

BookNest was one of my first full-fledged web app ideas — a platform where users could explore digital books in an organized and user-friendly layout. In this project, I didn’t just use HTML and CSS, but also experimented with JavaScript, PHP, and SQL to make the site more functional. I tried implementing features like book listings, categories, and simple database handling. Although it was a learning experience with many challenges, it helped me understand the importance of logic, backend structure, and how different technologies connect to make a website dynamic. Now, as I revisit web development from the basics, I see even more clearly how much I still have to learn — and that excites me. BookNest remains a proud early step that motivates me to build even better things in the future.

🌙 Eid Greeting Website

This was a creative and heartwarming project where I designed a festive website to share Eid greetings in a visually appealing way. Built with just HTML and CSS, this project allowed me to focus fully on visual design, layout, and emotion through styling. I used soft colors, celebratory fonts, and thoughtful spacing to create a joyful, peaceful atmosphere. It reminded me how powerful simple web design can be when it’s made with care and intention. Even without complex logic, this project taught me how to bring a personal touch to web experiences.
